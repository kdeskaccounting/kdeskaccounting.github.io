## Who We Are

KDesk Accounting builds practical, audit-ready tools for finance teams dealing with ASC 606, ASC 842, and commission accounting.

Our templates are built by a CPA with hands-on experience in sales compensation, revenue recognition, and subledger accounting at high-growth SaaS companies.

## Our Philosophy

**No macros. No subscriptions. No six-figure contracts.**

Most commission accounting solutions cost $30k–$100k+ per year. For finance teams with 10–200 deals, that's overkill. You need something between "manual spreadsheet chaos" and "enterprise software you can't afford yet."

That's exactly where we live.

## What We Offer

- **ASC 606 / ASC 340-40** commission capitalization and amortization workbooks
- **Reconciliation tools** that tie your subledger to your GL
- **Practical guides** for month-end close, journal entries, and audit prep

Every template is pure Excel formulas — no macros, no dependencies, works everywhere.
