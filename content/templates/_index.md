Three Excel workbooks built for controllers and finance managers at Series A–C SaaS companies.

Each template is purpose-built for one workflow: commission capitalization under ASC 606, lease accounting under ASC 842, or startup runway and cash flow modeling. No generic spreadsheet bundles. Every workbook ships formula-complete with sample data, protected formula cells, and yellow input zones.

## Available Templates

### [ASC 606 Commission Capitalization Workbook](/templates/asc606/) — $79
Capitalizes and amortizes sales commissions under ASC 340-40 for SaaS companies with multi-year contracts. 50-deal capacity, three amortization methods, automated journal entries, and reconciliation.

### [ASC 842 Lease Accounting Workbook](/templates/asc842/) — $97
ROU asset, lease liability, amortization schedule, journal entries, rollforward, and disclosure table for operating and finance leases. 20-lease capacity. Non-chained PV formulas.

### [Startup Runway Calculator](/templates/runway/) — $49
12 to 48-month cash flow forecast with Base, Optimistic, and Pessimistic scenario modeling. KPI dashboard, five input tabs, annual growth rates, and frequency logic per line item.
