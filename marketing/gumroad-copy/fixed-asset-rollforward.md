## What's in the workbook

- **50-asset Register** with vouching anchors auditors will ask for: Invoice #, PO #, CapEx Approver, Approval Date
- **Four depreciation methods:** Straight-Line, Double-Declining Balance with automatic switch to SL, Sum-of-Years-Digits, Units of Production
- **120-month Schedule per asset** with running-sum architecture — no cascading errors when an input changes
- **In-Service Date distinct from Acquisition Date** — depreciation starts when the asset is placed in service, not when invoiced (ASC 360-10-30-1)
- **Leasehold Improvements** automatically capped at the shorter of useful life or remaining lease term (ASC 842-20-35-12)
- **JE Generator with GL system presets:** Generic / QuickBooks / NetSuite / Sage Intacct / Xero — column headers adjust to each system's required fields
- **Disposal Log** with gain/loss math, Authorization, Evidence Type (Bill of Sale, Scrap Receipt, Write-off Memo, Insurance Claim, Police Report), Evidence Reference, Counterparty
- **Commercial-Substance flag for trade-ins** — drives ASC 845-10-30 treatment automatically
- **ASC 250 Change Log tab** — useful life or method revisions tracked prospectively with prior value, new value, effective period, reason, and approver
- **Closed Periods table** — lock prior months so a stray edit doesn't silently restate audited periods
- **Policy table on Setup** with deviation flag — pre-built consistency test for your auditor
- **Performance Materiality + Significant Asset flag** — assets above threshold pre-flagged for 100% audit testing
- **Five-way Reconciliation tab** with diagnostic sub-section — Schedule = JE = Rollforward = Register = GL, with ranked likely causes for any variance and cell-jump hyperlinks
- **Audit Confirmation Export tab** — print-ready PBC format for your auditor's sample of 25
- **Hidden _Pivot tab** with pre-built PivotTable for slicing depreciation by category × period

## Out of scope (and clearly so)

This is book depreciation under US GAAP only. It does NOT cover MACRS / tax depreciation, §168(k) bonus, §179, ASC 360-10-35 impairment, componentized depreciation, Asset Retirement Obligations, or lessor accounting. A free-text "Tax book differs (memo)" column lets you cross-reference each asset to your tax provision prep, but the workbook does not compute tax depreciation. If your portfolio exceeds 50 assets, dedicated fixed-asset software (Sage Fixed Assets, Bloomberg Tax) earns its cost.
